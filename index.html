About Us
Explore your destination with us. A great adventure begins here.
At Manordaf we offer clean and comfortable, en-suite B&B accommodation in a large detached Victorian house set in its own grounds next to the River Taf.  Our guests enjoy comfortable beds, high quality bedding and delicious breakfasts cooked to order.  We pride ourselves on offering a warm and friendly welcome and try to offer something a little extra to make our guests feel special. We provide in all rooms:- Tea, Coffee, Hot Chocolate & Milk A selection of luxury biscuits 32" Flat screen TVs as standard
